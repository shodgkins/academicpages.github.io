--- title: "FTIRbaselines (an R script for determining relative FTIR peak heights representing various organic compound classes)" excerpt: collection: portfolio websiteurl: "https://github.com/shodgkins/FTIRbaselines" --- https://github.com/shodgkins/FTIRbaselines This is an R script for determining relative peak heights from Fourier Transform Infrared Spectroscopy (FTIR), including peaks corresponding to carbohydrates, aromatics, aliphatics, and organic acids. For more information, please see the following publication: Hodgkins, S. B., Richardson, C. J., Dommain, R., Wang, H., Glaser, P. H., Verbeke, B., Winkler, B. R., Cobb, A. R., Rich, V.
